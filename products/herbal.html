Herbal Extracts.

A dedicated, GMP-certified extraction facility that processes around 60 tonnes of raw herbs every month into standardised extracts.

What we make

Standardised botanical extracts produced to consistent marker-compound levels, supplied as liquids or spray-dried powders to suit your formulation.

  • Water and hydro-alcoholic extraction
  • Standardised to defined active / marker content
  • Available as concentrates or free-flowing dried powders

Applications

Food and flavour, pharmaceutical and nutraceutical industries — for functional formulations, supplements and fortified products.

Quality

Made on imported equipment and verified in our in-house laboratory. Every batch is standardised on site. Share the botanical and standardisation you need and we’ll send the matching spec.
