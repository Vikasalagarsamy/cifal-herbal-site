Quality

Standardised, tested, traceable.

Every extract is standardised to its active compound and verified in our in-house laboratory (Shimadzu HPLC, GC and UV). Each batch is tested for active content, heavy metals, microbial limits and residues, with a COA available on request.

Typical standards
Heavy metals
Within pharmacopeial limits (Pb ≤ 10 ppm, As ≤ 3 ppm, Cd ≤ 1 ppm, Hg ≤ 0.1 ppm)
Microbial limits
TPC ≤ 10³ CFU/g · Yeast & Mould ≤ 10² CFU/g · Pathogens absent
Shelf life
24–36 months (cool, dry, away from direct sunlight)
Packaging
25 kg fibre / HDPE drums with inner liner · custom packing available
