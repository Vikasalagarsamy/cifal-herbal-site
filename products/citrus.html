Citrus Lime Products.

The heart of Cifal Herbal — value-added ingredients extracted from garden-fresh limes, with every part of the fruit converted into a useful product.

What we make

  • Lime pectin — rapid-set and slow-set grades for gelling and stabilising
  • Lime oil — cold-pressed peel oil for flavour and fragrance
  • Lime juice & concentrate — for beverages and food applications
  • Dried lime peel — for extraction and flavour
  • Citrus fibre — natural functional fibre
  • Calcium citrate — for fortification and nutraceutical use

Applications

Food and beverage manufacturers, flavour houses, pharmaceutical and nutraceutical companies in India and abroad rely on these ingredients for gelling, stabilising, flavouring and fortification.

Quality

Produced on imported lines and verified batch-by-batch in our in-house laboratory (Shimadzu HPLC, GC and UV). Available against your specification — talk to us about grade, packing and volume.
