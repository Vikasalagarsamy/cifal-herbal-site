Spray Dried Powders.

The juice or pulp of most fruits and vegetables, converted into free-flowing powder — with flavour, colour and taste kept intact.

What we make

  • Fruit juice & pulp powders
  • Vegetable powders
  • Custom blends to your flavour and colour brief

Why spray-dried

Spray drying converts liquids into stable, free-flowing powders that are easy to store, transport and dose — while retaining the natural flavour, colour and taste of the source.

Applications

Instant mixes, bakery, confectionery, beverages, seasonings and nutraceutical formulations. Available against your specification for particle size, solubility and packing.
