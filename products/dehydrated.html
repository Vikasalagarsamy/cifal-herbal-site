Dehydrated Fruits & Vegetables.

Osmotic-dehydrated fruits — developed with the Indian Institute of Horticultural Research — and conventionally dried vegetables.

What we make

  • Osmotic-dehydrated fruits — a process developed with the IIHR that preserves taste and texture
  • Conventionally dried vegetables — for soups, mixes and seasonings

Why osmotic dehydration

Osmotic dehydration removes moisture gently, helping retain the natural flavour, colour and nutritional value of the fruit while extending shelf life.

Applications

Snacking, bakery, cereal and confectionery, instant meal kits and seasoning blends. Available in a range of cuts and pack sizes to your specification.
