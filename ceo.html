From the Managing Director

A message from our Managing Director.

“Welcome to Cifal Herbal.”

We began as a food-processing venture built on the lime fruit. Our location in Gudur, Andhra Pradesh, gives us reliable access to the finest raw material throughout the year.

From the start our principle has been simple: generate no waste. Every component of the lime fruit is transformed into a useful product, using only natural ingredients.

We have invested in advanced equipment from Mexico, the USA and Italy, supported by a comprehensive R&D laboratory, to meet international quality standards. Our range has grown to include spray-dried juice powders and a dedicated herbal-extraction service, allowing us to serve diverse industrial sectors.

Over the years we have earned the loyalty of major multinational clients and received national recognition, including the 2007 National Award from the Government of India’s MSME division.

Our commitment remains unchanged — to deliver international-quality, natural products while keeping the values we were founded on.

Vidyasagar Yalavarthy
Managing Director, Cifal Herbal Pvt. Ltd.
