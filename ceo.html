CEO’s Message

A message from our Managing Director.

Vidyasagar Yalavarthy
Managing Director

Welcome to Cifal Herbal.

Our journey began as a food-processing enterprise focused on value-added products derived from lime fruit. We are strategically positioned in Gudur, Andhra Pradesh — India’s primary citrus belt — which gives us reliable access to the finest raw material throughout the year.

From the very beginning we set ourselves a simple discipline: generate no waste. Every part of the lime fruit is converted into a useful by-product, and we work only with natural ingredients. It is a philosophy that has shaped every investment and process decision we have made since.

To meet international standards, we have equipped our unit with machinery imported from Mexico, the USA and Italy, supported by a fully equipped R&D laboratory. Over the years we have expanded into spray-dried juice powders and a dedicated herbal extraction facility, allowing us to serve a far wider range of industries.

These efforts have earned the trust of leading multinational companies, as well as recognition such as the 2007 National Award from the Government of India’s MSME division.

As we look ahead, our commitment remains unchanged: to offer international-quality, natural products while staying true to the values on which this company was built. Thank you for taking the time to learn about us.

Vidyasagar Yalavarthy
Managing Director, Cifal Herbal Pvt. Ltd.
