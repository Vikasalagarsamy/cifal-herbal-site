The company

Processing citrus limes and botanicals since 1985.

Cifal Herbal transforms fresh limes and botanical materials into natural ingredients for the food, flavour, pharmaceutical and nutraceutical sectors — from a single integrated facility in Gudur, Andhra Pradesh.

Profile

A four-decade-old citrus processor in India’s main lime belt.

Established in 1985 (formerly Southern Citrus Private Ltd.), Cifal Herbal Private Limited manufactures pectin, citrus fibre, lime oil and spray-dried powders, alongside a GMP-certified herbal-extraction facility.

Everything is produced on dedicated, in-house lines with on-site quality control, on a 4.5-acre green-field site at Gudur, in the Nellore district near Chennai on the national highway.
