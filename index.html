The company

Cifal Herbal manufactures almost every value-added product that can be derived from the citrus lime.

Pectin, lime oil, lime juice, dried peel, citrus fibre and calcium citrate — all produced on a 4.5-acre site in India’s main lime belt at Gudur, Andhra Pradesh, formerly Southern Citrus Private Ltd.

A dedicated herbal extraction facility processes around 60 tonnes of raw herbs each month into standardised extracts for the food, flavour, pharmaceutical and nutraceutical industries.

How we work

Process and quality controls.

01

Zero waste

Every part of the lime — peel, juice and oil — is converted into a useful product. Nothing is discarded.

02

Imported equipment

Crushing, extraction and drying lines imported from Mexico, the USA and Italy, run to international standards.

03

In-house laboratory

Shimadzu HPLC, GC and UV spectrophotometers verify and standardise every batch on site.

04

Only natural products

Every line is made from natural ingredients — no synthetic substitutes anywhere.
